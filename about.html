About

This site publishes practical comparisons of WordPress hosting for agencies managing multiple client sites. The goal is simple: help you pick a hosting provider that reduces downtime, support churn, and operational babysitting.

We focus on agency workflows (staging, backups, migrations, roles) and predictable scaling. We don’t do “best hosting” for everyone.
