Frequently Asked Questions

Is managed WordPress hosting worth it for agencies?

Yes — once you manage 10+ client sites, the time saved on maintenance, backups, and emergencies usually outweighs the higher monthly cost.

Why not VPS or shared hosting?

These options often shift operational risk onto the agency. Managed hosting trades cost for predictability.

How many sites before managed hosting makes sense?

Most agencies feel the break-even point around 8–12 active client sites.
